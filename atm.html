What is ATM about

ATM stands for Applied Technology Magnet. It's a program for middle schoolers. It's a three year program. Student's 6th-8th learn new way of using a computer or other apps. For example coding. A lot of kids in Bancroft Middle School do applied magnet technology. Here is a link to my teacher Miss. Milton website about ATM. The link is in All About ATM! Enjoy.
